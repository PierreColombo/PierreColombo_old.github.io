About me

Im am a first year PhD student at Télécom Paris, Institut Polytechnique de Paris, under the supervision of Chloé Clavel (Télécom Paris), Giovanna Varni (Télécom Paris) and Emmanuel Vignon (IBM). I am working on various fields of Natural Language with a strong emphasis on Natural Language Generation. I held two MSc in Computer Science from Supélec (France) and EPFL (Switzerland).
